About Us

Long time friends Dave and Roli are the creative duo behind High Vibes. The idea sprouted gradually as they were creating projects for fun and noticed the success that came from their synergy. High Vibes is an effort to formalize their endeavors for a bigger impact.

Rolando Mathias
Designer

While legally a Swede, Roli is a born and bred New Yorker. He recently returned to Brooklyn after several years in Stockholm where he designed for a few Swedish companies. He led the design for the fintech app that introduced credit scores to Swedish consumers. He also co-founded Primablock in 2017. David Lilja
Photographer

Dave grew up all over Sweden and has spent the last decade based in NYC as a fashion model. He has done campaigns in all of the major fashion markets. Along the way, Dave developed an interest in and talent for creative direction, and in particular, photography. He shoots fashion models, commercial products, and landscapes, but his main talent is empathizing with clients to understand their vision.
